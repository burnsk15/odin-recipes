Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

The most amzing soft chocolate chip cookies you will ever have

This is the absolute most amazing recipe for soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies. I like to make mine with dark chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter melted and cooled
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1.5 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Add sugar and melted butter and mix until creamy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla and mix until just combined.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt and mix until just combined. be careful not to over mix.
  5. Form in larger balls, about 3 tbsps worth, and bake 8-10 minutes long. They will seem pale and puffy but believe.
  6. Let sit on the cookie sheet for 30 minutes and they will be perfect.
