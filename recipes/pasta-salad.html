Caprese Pasta Salad

Healthy pasta salad

This pasta salad can be made with whole wheat pasta making it a little extra healthier as we get produce, fats, and healthy carbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb whole wheat pasta
  • 1 container of grape tomatoes, cut in halves or quarters.
  • Small handfull of fresh basil leave, cut into strips
  • 8 oz Mozzerella pearls
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 TBSP dried basil
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to directions on the box. Drain and rinse with cold water until cooled.
  2. While pasta cooks, cut the tomatoes and basil.
  3. Add all ingredients into a large bowl and stir, or into a 1 gallon ziplock bag and shake until everything is covered in herbs and olive oil.
  4. Enjoy!
