Meat Soup

This amazing soup is packed with protein, and is great over rice.

This soup is made with bacon, sausage, and shirmp. It is is a mexican inspired soup that is usually served over some rice with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb bacon
  • 1 pack of smoked sausage
  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 2 diced jalapenos
  • 1 can of petite diced tomatos
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • handful of cilantro chopped
  • 2 limes
  • salt to taste
  • 2 packets od sazon
  • 2 tsp chili powder

Steps

  1. Cut bacon into bite sized peices and start cooking on a medium heat.
  2. once bacon is cooked add in onions and jalapenos cooking until onions are translucent.
  3. Add garlic and cook stiring for 30 seconds. Add sausages making sure to stir occasionally as they heat up and get some color.
  4. Add canned tomatos, with juice. Add sazon and chili powder making sure to stir. Heat until simmering.
  5. Simmer for 2 minutes and add thawed shrimp making sure to submerge all the shrimp in the liquid. Cover and heat for 2 minutes before stiring and repeating until all shrimp are cooked through.
  6. Take off heat. Add juice of 2 limes and the chopped cilantro making sure to stir again. Serve over rice.
